The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is a franchise cricket team based in chennai, Tamil Nadu. being crowd favourites in the Indian Premier League (IPL), they decided to rebrand their website and its front end functionalities to create a more immersive experience for fans.

Understanding the challenge

The key challenge was to understand user behavior and craft a beautiful front end experience for avid fans. The website design and user experience had to capture the emotion of team spirit and solidarity.

Understanding the challenge

The key challenge was to understand user behavior and craft a beautiful front end experience for avid fans. The website design and user experience had to capture the emotion of team spirit and solidarity.

Rebranding

Being CSK fans ourselves made it easy to understand the emotion evoked by the brand. To analyze further, we tracked customer journeys on their existing website and gained key insights for rebranding. Once we had clarity on the user experience, we started with the wireframes and decided to retain CSK’s official brand color palette.

Typography & Colours

Proxima nova is an elegant, readable font featuring a true cursive Semibold. It was chosen based on its approachable, open, and friendly typeface that essentially produces the same visual effect both on screen and in print.
