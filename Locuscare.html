About Locuscare

LocusCare is a localization-based healthcare platform uniting doctors,labs,pharmacies, clinics, and patients to forma healthy and self-sufficient ecosystem. It brings all the users onto a single platform, making it easy and hassle-free for users to take care of their personal and family health at any time.

Challenge

Cognizant of the untapped potential in the digital Health space, LocusCare decided to bridge the gap between medical professionals, pharmacies and patients using technology. They wanted to build a platform which caters to the need of all parties involved, offering a hassle free user experience.
