Healthcare Experience

Managedcare

Managedcare is a versatile platform that allows professionals to coordinate and administrate a variety of healthcare services. Its seamless integration brings out affordability while delivering the best quality. This also translates into a better quality of service and access on the end-user, the patients’ ⁠— where they can reliable demand and get treated without any hassle.

Platforms:
 Web & Mobile

Deliverables:
 UI,UX, Brand & Strategy
Accept the

Challenge

Healthcare platforms and services until now have been quite fragmented. Every other solution to this challenge has either caused more fragmentation or brought about unnecessary complications. This also resulted in huge add-ups of time on the patient’s end, who end up getting their urgent care delayed by huge margins.

Our objective was to build a platform economy that solves the above problems and innovates healthcare in general. This platform economy would be a one-stop outlet that every provider can use to not just offer their services but also coordinate/collaborate with others.

Our solution also aimed at digitally transforming age-old paper-based systems that just made everything sluggish and put lives at risk.All of this meant that our solution needed to be advanced, adaptive, and easy-to-use for the end-user.
